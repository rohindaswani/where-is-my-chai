--- layout: default title: Where is my chai ---

Serves: 1

Cooking time: Don't worry, drink chai.

Base Ingredients:

  • 3 tbsp loose tea leaves
  • 2 cups of Milk (2% or whole milk)
  • 1 cup of water
  • Add one of the below

Ginger chai:

  • 1/2 inch Ginger crushed with a mortar/pestle or knife

Cardamom chai:

  • 4 cardamom pods crushed with a mortar/pestle or knife

Ginger/Lemongrass:

  • 1/2 inch of Ginger crushed with a mortar/pestle or knife
  • 1/2 inch of lemongrass cut and crushed with a mortar/pestle or knife

Instructions:

Add the milk, water, tea and spices to a pot. Stir it properly so that the ingredients get a chance to mix. Set the gas on medium/high heat and bring it to a boil. Let the mixture cook for approx. 10 - 15 minutes. When it turns a chocolate brown in color, take it off the gas and strain the tea leaves and spices. Add some sugar. I usually add 2 tbsp. If you are feeling fancy, sprinkle some saffron on top. Drink it with some marie gold biscuit.
